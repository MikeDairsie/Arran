Data:

Arran_Study_Generalised = Hydrology analysis
Arran_Study_Shapefiles = Monuments & white out areas used to mask areas of woodland and gorse
Arran_Study_Styles = QGIS styles for shapefiles
Arran_Study_Images = sample outputs showing how the data looks when loaded (note: lidar not included)

All monuments transcribed from lidar phase 2 tiles from https://remotesensingdata.gov.scot/
